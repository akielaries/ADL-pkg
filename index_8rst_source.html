1 Welcome to openMTPK's documentation.
2 ===================================
3 **openMTPK** is a open-source mathematics package written in C++ with language bindings available
4 for Fortran, OCaml, Python, and R. View the
5 The goal is to make a reusable mathematics library allowing users to call within their own projects.
6 Starting with some implementations of basics of different mathematical topics like arithmetic,
7 calculus, statistics, linear algebra, etc. in conjunction with more advanced algorithms seen in
8 the blend of such topics, branches of Machine Learning, Image Processing and much more. Some of
9 the modules operate in a circular dependant way, for example arithmetic operations seen in linear
10 algebra vector operations that can be seen in algorithms implemented into the deep learning module.
11 Many of these implementations were first prototypes in Wolfram Mathetmatica, then converted to C++
12 code for the package. Look in the drivers folder for examples on using these tools in your
13 own project. This paper will highlight the modules in use, the mathematics/logic behind
14 them, future developments, and more.
